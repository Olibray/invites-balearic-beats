The Concept

A rooftop set, filmed properly.

We invite guest DJs to play free sunset sets on the rooftops of two private villas in Cala Sant Vicenç. Drone and multi-camera crews film the set Cercle-style — sweeping aerials, golden light, a minimal crowd, the location and the music doing the work. The result is a standalone film, plus clips, the artist can put straight on their own channels.

Invited only

No open booking, no submissions queue. We approach artists whose sound fits the villas and the light.

Free to play

No fee changes hands. The trade is the film itself — high production, fully edited, and the audience that comes with it.

Cinematic production

Drone, GoPro and multi-camera coverage, professionally edited — built to be watched, not just streamed once and forgotten.

Content brand

This isn't a ticketed event. It's a film series — released as standalone episodes and short-form clips.

The Locations

Two rooftops.
Two moods.

Both villas sit in Cala Sant Vicenç on Mallorca's north coast, both with on-site accommodation for the artist and crew. Each plays as its own distinct set location.

Sea View

The Sea House

Golden hour over turquoise water. Rooftop terrace facing the Mediterranean, on-site rooms for artist and crew.

Mountain View

The Mountain House

Warm dusk tones against the Serra de Tramuntana. A quieter, more intimate rooftop — same crew, same standard.

For Artists

How it works.

01

Invited artists only

We reach out directly to DJs whose sound and presence fit the villas. There's no open application process.

02

Full production handled

Villa, crew, drone, multi-camera setup, and edit — all covered. You show up and play; we handle everything else.

03

Free to play

No fee changes hands either way. The trade is the film and the exposure it brings through the Balearic Beats audience.

04

You keep the rights

The finished film is yours to use across your own channels — full usage rights, no exclusivity beyond the initial release.
